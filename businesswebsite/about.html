About Us

The Aomori Applestore was founded by Trevor Masse. Trevor participated in the JET Programme, teaching English in Japan in the northern Tohoku region. Aomori apples are an iconic produce of the region. The Aomori Applestore brings the freshness and uncomparable taste of Japan to you!
